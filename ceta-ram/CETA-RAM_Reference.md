## Technical Notes

### Metal Structure Treatment
- Paint iron structures to prevent rust
- Remove loose rust with wire brush, sandpaper, or chemical rust remover
- Sand chipping paint areas until smooth
- Remove dust/oil with degreaser or denatured alcohol
- Prime surface to protect against rust and corrosion

### Wood Treatment
- Treat wood with pesticides and kiln-dry for adequate humidity
- Apply wood sealer for later use

### Imperial Conversion Notes
All metric steel sizes have been mapped to the nearest standard US imperial equivalent.
Rod/tube OD/ID pairs are matched so pins fit through bushings (e.g., 1-1/4" rod in
1-1/2" OD tube). Plate thicknesses: 13mm→1/2", 10mm→3/8", 6mm→1/4". C-channel:
152x52x8→C6x8.2. Bolts: M12/M14→1/2"-13. Brick size: 12" x 6" x ~4".
